Bang for buck explained

We rate upgrade proposals by comparing the percentage performance gain versus the percentage of added cost. The ratio is expressed as a percent and graded A–D.

  • Performance gain is based on your build’s CPU+GPU improvement.
  • Added cost is normalized by typical CPU+GPU prices using catalog averages (middle 60% to reduce outlier skew).
  • A and B indicate strong value, C is fair value, and D means the gain likely isn’t worth the cost.
